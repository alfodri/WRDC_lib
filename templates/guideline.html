Guidelines for New Authors

Introduction

Welcome to the Library! We are excited to have you join us as an author. This page provides the guidelines and steps you need to follow to get your publications added to our library.

Step 1: Register as an Author

To start, you need to register as an author. Please provide the following information:

  • Your full name
  • A profile picture (optional but recommended)
  • Your CV including your education, experience, and technical skills

Submit this information through our Admin Panel.

Step 2: Prepare Your Publication

Make sure your publication meets the following requirements:

  • The document is in PDF format
  • The cover image is in JPEG or PNG format
  • All images are of high quality

Step 3: Submit Your Publication

Once your publication is ready, log in to the Admin Panel and submit the following details:

  • Title of the publication
  • Author name
  • Category of the publication
  • Publish date
  • PDF document
  • Cover image

After submission, your publication will be reviewed and added to the library if it meets all the requirements.

Author Skills

We value the following skills in our authors:

  • Advanced English proficiency
  • Academic writing
  • Appropriate Level of Citation
